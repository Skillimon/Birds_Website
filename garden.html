Practical tips

  • Place feeders near cover so birds can shelter quickly; keep feeders clean to prevent disease.
  • Put a shallow dish with a rough surface or a few stones for butterflies to puddle and drink.
  • Install a few nest boxes of different sizes and heights, and clean them in autumn.
  • Leave seed heads and a patch of long grass through winter â€” it feeds birds and supports insect life.
  • Keep a small area of the garden a little wild â€” log piles and a nettle patch support invertebrates.

Quick checklist

  • Food: feeders + berry-producing plants
  • Water: bird bath or small pond
  • Shelter: dense shrubs, ivy, hedges
  • Breeding: nest boxes and native trees
  • Butterflies: nectar plants + nettle patch
